About the florist

Hany is certified in flower arranging from the New York Botanical Garden. She was born in Morocco, grew up in Israel, and has lived in New York for 34 years. She developed a passion for graphic design at a young age which translated to skills in flower arranging. She loves country music, her two sons, and all kinds of flowers, especially tropical varieties.
