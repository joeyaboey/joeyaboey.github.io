Ticket Options

VIP

Enjoy the luxurious experience of VIP. This ticket will grant you access to all events via VIP lane. You will enjoy shorter lines and better viewing sections. If we run into rain during the event you have access to a covered lounge area that is equiped with TV's to view the event and food and Beverages.The best part is you will have access to VIP restrooms. Theses restrooms will be fully equipped bathrooms with mirrors and sinks.

Preferred

Preferred entry will allow you to access the line via a designated lane seperate from general admission.tickets holders will get advanced entry to the venue before general admission ticket owners. You will also have these privilleges at the food tents. This level of access will also allow you access to separate restroom section designated for preferred ticket holders.

General Admission

General Admission grants you entry to everyday of the festival. Enjoy the music of your favorite artist and stretch out on the great lawn. Entry to the festival starts for genenral admission at 10AM.

Day Pass

This pass is valid for any one day of general admission entry into the festival. After it is used you will have to get another pass if you choose to go to the festival another day.
